About me

With over 10 years of experience in BIM, VDC, 4D and 5D modeling, and design of concrete structures, I am a BIM Manager at ASASS Construction, a leading construction company in the region. I have earned the RICS and Autodesk certifications, demonstrating my proficiency and professionalism in BIM technology and standards.

I enjoy working with and leading a team of talented engineers, and I am always eager to learn new skills and technologies. I have strong skills in team building, team leadership, and design review, and I have supported and guided junior engineers in their tasks and development. I am passionate about BIM and its potential to improve the efficiency and quality of construction projects.
